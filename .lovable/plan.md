

# Lettzo Landing Page Plan

## Overview
A modern, premium landing page for **Lettzo** - a social meetup app that helps people stop doing things alone and connect through real-life plans.

---

## Design System

### Colors (from logo)
- **Primary**: Vibrant red/coral (CTAs, highlights, icons)
- **Secondary**: Bold black (text, headers)
- **Background**: Clean white with subtle gradients
- **Accents**: Soft grays for cards and sections

### Typography
- **Headlines**: Satoshi (bold, confident, modern)
- **Body text**: Inter (clean, readable)

### Style
- Gen-Z friendly, high-trust startup aesthetic
- Smooth gradients, soft shadows, rounded cards
- Subtle hover animations and transitions
- Mobile-first, generous whitespace

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Lettzo logo (left)
- Minimal nav links: Features, How it Works, About
- "Get Started" CTA button (red)

### 2. Hero Section (Above the fold)
- **Headline**: "Stop Doing Things Alone"
- **Subtext**: "Coffee runs. Gym sessions. Movie nights. Travel adventures. Find people who want to do the same things—right now."
- **Primary CTA**: "Create a Plan" → Opens Coming Soon modal
- **Secondary CTA**: "Explore Nearby" → Opens Coming Soon modal
- **Visual**: Full-width lifestyle image of young adults socializing in an urban setting

### 3. Core Features Section
Icon cards showcasing:
- ⚡ **Create Plans in Seconds** - Set up spontaneous or scheduled meetups
- 🗺️ **Discover Nearby** - Find plans on an interactive map
- 💬 **Group Chat Built-in** - Every plan has its own chat
- 👥 **Friends & Strangers** - Meet new people or stick with your circle
- 🏷️ **Interest Badges** - Show what you're into

### 4. Map Discovery Section
- Visual mockup of a map interface
- Emphasis on "See who's down to hang out near you"
- Lifestyle imagery of spontaneous meetups

### 5. Social Connection Section
- Emotional messaging about real-world connections
- "Because scrolling alone gets old" vibe
- Imagery of people laughing, walking, connecting IRL

### 6. Social Proof / Stats Section
- Placeholder stats or community highlights
- Optional testimonial cards (with placeholder content)

### 7. Final CTA Section
- Bold call-to-action: "Ready to stop doing things alone?"
- Email signup teaser or "Coming Soon" button
- Gradient background using brand colors

### 8. Footer
- Lettzo logo
- Links: About, Privacy, Terms, Contact
- Social media icons (placeholder)
- Copyright notice

---

## Interactive Elements

### Coming Soon Modal
When users click any CTA button:
- Clean modal overlay
- "Lettzo is launching soon!"
- Optional email input for waitlist (visual only, no backend)
- Animated confetti or subtle celebration effect

---

## Imagery
Professional lifestyle photos from Unsplash/Pexels featuring:
- Young adults in cafés, gyms, urban settings
- Natural lighting, cinematic feel
- Diverse, authentic-looking people socializing
- NO cheesy stock photos or dating-app vibes

---

## Technical Approach
- Responsive design (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll behavior
- CSS animations for hover states and section reveals
- Google Fonts: Satoshi + Inter
